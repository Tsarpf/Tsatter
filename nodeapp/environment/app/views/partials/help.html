Link an image and it should automatically open next to the chat. All chats have infinite chat history. That means you can just scroll up and you'll get the whole history (if any exists) for the channel since the beginning of time. You can link messages. Right click the message timestamp and copy its URL. When someone opens that link, they'll automatically join the channel and their chat will be scrolled to the message you linked, and the message will be highlighted. If you're the first to join a channel (either just empty at the moment or completely new), you get moderator privileges. This is indicated by your nick being in bold This means you can kick people out of the channel or give other people moderator privileges. You can (if you're a mod) either use /op "nick" or /kick "nick" or just click on their nickname in the user list on the left. Channels are based on IRC (that is, they run on an actual IRC server). If you're familiar with IRC protocol, you can send IRC commands straight to the IRC-server by putting a / at the start of your message. I've also added some shortcuts for the commands that are usually found in IRC-clients. The commands at the moment are /kick /op /deop /part
